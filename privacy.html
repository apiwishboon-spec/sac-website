Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 10, 2026

1. Introduction

Suankularb Astronomy Club ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information when you visit our website or make purchases from our shop.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following information:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, and shipping address when you place an order.
  • Payment Information: Payment slip images for verification of bank transfers.
  • Order Information: Products purchased, order date, and order status.
  • Technical Information: Browser type, IP address, and device information for security purposes.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Process and fulfill your orders
  • Send order confirmations and updates via email
  • Verify payment through bank transfer slips
  • Contact you regarding your order if needed
  • Improve our services and website experience
  • Prevent fraud and ensure security

4. Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. Your information is only shared with:

  • ImgBB (image hosting service) for temporary storage of payment slip images
  • Google Services (Apps Script, Sheets) for order processing
  • Law enforcement if required by legal obligation

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information:

  • Cloudflare Turnstile verification to prevent spam
  • Email verification (OTP) for order confirmation
  • Secure data storage in Google Workspace
  • Limited access to order data by authorized club staff only

6. Data Retention

We retain your order information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy, typically for the duration of the academic year plus one additional year for record-keeping purposes. Payment slip images are automatically deleted after 60 days.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your data (subject to legal requirements)
  • Withdraw consent for marketing communications

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us:

  • Email: skastronomy.club@gmail.com
  • Facebook: Suankularb Astronomy Club
